Breaking News: Crazed Man Murder's Wife

Just last night a man named Marcus Maverick murdered his wife in their city home, and entombed her in a cellar wall.

Police reports claim that Marcus taunted them by proclaiming the structural soundness of the house, going as far

as to tap the wall in which his wife, Minnie Maverick, was encased behind. However, this taunting would prove to

be his own undoing. Police reports say that after Marcus tapped the wall with his cane, a "discordant screeching

erupted from the wall". Upon hearing this, police tore down the wall only to reveal the Maverick's cat sitting

atop the corpse of Minnie, covered in her blood.


Upon seeing this, Marcus began yelling incoherent claims regarding the cat. As Marcus was carried out of his house,

neighbors reported hearing him claim that "This damned cat is the spawn of satan," and "I didn't kill Minnie.

This creature caused everything."
