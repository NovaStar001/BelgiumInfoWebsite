AffinityCraft SMP

Our current season revolves around an industrial theme, featuring the Create mod as the primary focus. In addition to the Create mod, players can enjoy the enhanced resource gathering provided by Timber, and encounter a diverse array of creatures with Alex's Mobs, making every adventure on AffinityCraft SMP dynamic and engaging.

We are actively seeking new members to join our community! To become a part of AffinityCraft SMP, simply hop onto our Discord server and complete an application. We look forward to welcoming you to our server!
